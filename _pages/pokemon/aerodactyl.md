# Aerodactyl

Aerodactyl is a dual-type Rock/Flying Fossil Pokémon.

It is resurrected from an Old Amber, and is not known to evolve into or from any
other Pokémon.

## Location

Aerodactyl is ressurrected from an Old Amber on Cinnabar Island.
