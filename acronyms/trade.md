# Trading Acronyms

The following Acronyms are commonly used in Trade and are useful to understand:

* `WTS`: Want to Sell
* `WTB`: Want to Buy
* `WTT`: Want to Trade
* `PC`: This can be either Price Check or Pokémon Center, depending on context.
